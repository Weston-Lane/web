  Hello!
  To give a little background, I am from a small town in Texas have always tinkered with computers. Out of highschool I joined the United States Coast Guard. First tour was as a non-rated Fireman at USCG smallboat station Fort Pierce. There I preformed routine maintenance on our sea craft, conducted law enforcement missions, and search and rescue operations. Later my training led me to become a 3rd class Avionics Electrical Technician where my job was to troubleshoot, coordinate, and conduct maintenace on USCG aircraft. I also operated the in flight radar for law enforcement and search and rescue operations.
 While in service I attended a local community college where I took Computer Science courses. After Seperating I decided to pursue my higher education further at Rutgers University-New Brunswick where I am today. In my free time you can catch me working on personal projects. I like to make small games or low level graphics tech. My interest in the field stems from my fascination in the process of bringing virtual worlds to life.
Outside of school and graphics I like to play guitar, video games, and read. I try to go outside sometimes too.
